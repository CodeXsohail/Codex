Rules and Regulations

1. General Rules
  • The test will be conducted in three levels.
  • Test Fee: Rs. 100 must be submitted before the test begins.
  • Candidates who participate will receive a medal.
  • Top 40% of candidates from Level 1 will proceed to Level 2.
  • Participants in Level 2 will receive a certificate.
  • Top 5 candidates in Level 3 will be awarded prizes.
2. Syllabus Instructions

Level 1:

  • Objective-type questions from the academic syllabus.
  • Emphasis on conceptual clarity and deep understanding.

Level 2:

  • Medium-difficulty objective questions.
  • Coding questions requiring logical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Level 3:

  • Advanced coding questions designed to test programming expertise and efficiency.
3. Test Rules and Regulations
  • Maintain complete silence during the test.
  • Talking, whispering, or signaling is strictly prohibited.
  • Misbehavior with invigilators or peers will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Cheating will lead to expulsion from the test.
  • Using unauthorized materials (e.g., mobile phones, notes, or electronic devices) is prohibited.
  • Participants are not allowed to leave the test hall until the test is completed.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators.
  • Raise your hand if you have technical issues, but no test-related questions will be entertained.
  • Seating will be assigned, and candidates must sit in their designated places.
4. Additional Information
  • Participation medals will be distributed at the end of Level 1.
  • Level 2 certificates will be given after the completion of that round.
  • Prizes for the top 5 candidates in Level 3 will be awarded during the closing ceremony.
  • By participating in the CODEX Test, candidates agree to abide by all rules and regulations stated above.
  • Failure to adhere to these rules will result in immediate disqualification without a refund of the test fee.

Note: By participating in the CODEX Test, candidates agree to abide by all rules and regulations. Violations will result in disqualification without a refund of the test fee.
